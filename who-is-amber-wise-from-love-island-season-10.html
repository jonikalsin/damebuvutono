Who Is Amber Wise From Love Island Season 10?

The former Premier League star Dennis Wise’s daughter Amber is set to shock the Love Island mansion. The 19-year-old student is participating in season 10 of Love Island in the hopes of finding true love.

The return of Casa Amor, which will feature six shocking revelations, including Amber moving into the main villa, will startle fans. Dennis Wise, a former football player, has appeared on reality television in the past. Amber has 12.9k Instagram followers and is currently pursuing a degree in visual communication.

In the first episode of Love Island season 10, which debuted on June 5, Molly Marsh’s love interest Zachariah Noble was abruptly taken away by the bombshell Kady McDermott. On June 30, Love Island released a sneak peek at the upcoming episode, indicating that Casa Amor will return and that six new men and six new bombshells would join the existing gang.

Amber Wise is a student at the University of the Arts London

Amber Wise, who is currently a visual communication student at the University of the Arts London, is reputed to have a low-key, easygoing demeanor. She has previously played netball for the Surrey Storm U15 team. The insider observed that despite her cool demeanor, she is determined to reject all “nonsense.”

Amber and her father have a close relationship, and he fully supports her decision to compete. She reportedly longs for real love and wants to please her father. Like Amber, Dennis Wise has experience in front of reality television cameras, having appeared on ITV’s I’m A Celebrity Get Me Out Of Here in 2017. One of the last to depart the program was he.

The most recent Love Island contestant with celebrity parents is Amber Wise. Cooper Keating, Ronan Keating’s son, and Gemma Owen, Michael Owen’s daughter, both had appearances on the show in previous seasons.

How did Love Island tease the Casa Amor?

The one and only Ne-Yo delighted the Love Islanders with a nice surprise performance in the previous episode. The ladies were asked to take advantage of the chance to get their picture taken with the well-known singer. Ne-Yo led them to a Casa VIP minivan that was waiting to take them to Casa Amor after they took a group picture.

The show closed in traditional Love Island style, with a brief slow-motion montage of Casa Amor bombshells, just as the guys were figuring out where the girls had disappeared. One of them was Amber Wise.
