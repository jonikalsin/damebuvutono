Innocence Project Scott Peterson Wikipedia And Age: Is He Married?

The Innocence Project is looking into the case of Scott Peterson, who was convicted of murder in 2004! Attention seeking Scott Peterson wikipedia page provides background on his trial and conviction at age 31.

Scott Peterson, who was convicted in 2004 of murdering his pregnant wife Laci Peterson, and their unborn son Conner, is now being represented by the Los Angeles Innocence Project to prove his innocence.

Peterson reported Laci missing on Christmas Eve 2002; her body and that of their unborn son were later found in San Francisco Bay.

Peterson was convicted of first-degree murder in 2004 and sentenced to death in 2005.

In 2020, the California Supreme Court overturned his death sentence due to issues with the jury selection process but upheld his convictions.

In 2021 he was resentenced to life without parole.

In April 2023, Peterson’s new attorneys filed a habeas corpus petition alleging constitutional violations and actual innocence supported by new evidence.

They are seeking access to materials from the original investigation and trial to build their case.

The Los Angeles Innocence Project took on Peterson’s case after his death sentence was overturned, as he no longer qualified for a court-appointed lawyer.

They aim to eventually overturn his murder convictions entirely.

Innocence Project Scott Peterson Wikipedia

The Los Angeles Innocence Project is a non-profit organization founded in 2001 that works to exonerate wrongfully convicted individuals in California.

According to wikipedia, the organization reviews over 2,000 claims of innocence annually and determines which cases to fully investigate.

The Innocence Project has helped exonerate 30 wrongfully convicted people in California.

Nationwide, various Innocence Projects have helped exonerate over 375 wrongfully convicted people based on DNA evidence.

However, the Los Angeles Innocence Project handles both DNA and non-DNA cases.

For Scott Peterson, they are investigating non-DNA evidence that could prove his innocence.

The Los Angeles Innocence Project has a high bar for taking cases, only accepting those with strong evidence of innocence that was not presented at trial.

Their involvement indicates they believe Peterson has a credible claim that warrants thorough reinvestigation.

Scott Peterson Age

Scott Peterson was born on October 24, 1972, in San Diego, California. He was 30 years old at the time of his wife Laci’s disappearance in 2002.

Now 51 years old, Peterson has spent over 17 years of his adult life incarcerated following his conviction in 2004.

He is currently serving a life sentence without the possibility of parole at San Quentin State Prison.

At the time of Laci Peterson’s disappearance, the couple had been married for 4 years and were eagerly awaiting the birth of their first child, a son they planned to name Conner.

Tragically, neither Laci nor Conner would ever be found alive.

At 51, Peterson still maintains his innocence. His chance at exoneration rests on the efforts of the Los Angeles Innocence Project to uncover new evidence that could vacate his convictions.

They have a challenging road ahead to prove his conviction was wrongful after all these years.

Is Scott Peterson Married?

Scott Peterson is currently not married. His only marriage was to Laci Peterson, his high school sweetheart whom he married in 1997 after meeting her at California Polytechnic State University.

Their marriage lasted 5 years until Laci tragically disappeared while pregnant with their first child in December 2002.

Peterson infamously reported her missing on Christmas Eve, triggering a nationwide search.

Laci’s disappearance and the subsequent revelation of Peterson’s extramarital affair dominated headlines at the time and captivated the country.

Peterson was convicted of murdering Laci and their unborn son in 2004 and sentenced to death in 2005.

After nearly 20 years in prison, he maintains his innocence despite overwhelming evidence of his guilt presented at trial.

Peterson’s short-lived marriage to Laci came to a devastating end over 17 years ago when she was killed.

Today he remains single, incarcerated at San Quentin State Prison, as he continues to claim he did not murder his pregnant wife.
