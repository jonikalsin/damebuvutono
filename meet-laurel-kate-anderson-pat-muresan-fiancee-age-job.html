Who Is Laurel Kate Anderson Late Boyfriend?

They demanded $10 million while blaming the county jail for their son’s death on the “negligent, reckless” actions of sheriff’s deputies.

“It’s just not real to me,” she admitted. “I feel like Chris is out of town. Or… I just haven’t seen him for a while. I can’t wrap my head around the fact that I won’t see him again in this life.”

Chris and Laurel were in a relationship when he passed away on 17 February 2017.

Chris called Flowery Branch his hometown which was also his residence in his last days. Christopher received education from Johnson High School and Gainesville State College. He worked as an accountant at RWH Trucking.
