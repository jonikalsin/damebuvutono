Get To Know Blue Frog Ranch's Jamie Dube! Bio, Age, Net Worth, Married

Duane Ollinger’s sprawling 160-acre property in Utah’s Uintah Basin continued to reveal its mystery as Discovery Blind Frog Ranch renewed for an all-new season on 7 January 2022. The reality series follows Duane Ollinger, the father, and Chad Ollinger, the son, as they hunt for treasure on their Blind Frog Ranch. However, there are also many others making a significant impact in this treasure hunt. One of them is Jamie Dube (of course).

Now, the rest of this ‘Jamie Dube Bio’ is where you shall read Jamie’s age, net worth, married life, and Blind Frog Ranch-related stories.

Jamie Dube On Blind Frog Ranch

Jamie Dube joins Duane, his son Chad, who is the head of ranch security Charlie Snider, world-renowned geologist Eric Drummond, in their quest for Aztec gold.

Like everyone in the team, Jamie is also convinced that other than the Lost Rhodes Mines Gold there is some big paranormal activity hidden inside the caverns of the Utah Mountains.

(Of course) this is not the first time Jamie is out hunting and investigating the paranormal.

A former police officer-turned-real estate agent, he founded East Coast Ghost Trackers sometime ago. East Coast, if you should know, is a group that has led guided ghost tours at Fort Knox (a Maine’s resource guide company) for years.

Confident there will be spirits at night, he and Ken Ort had founded the company.

Around that time, Jamie also published a book, “Where Do You Walk?” penned about getting into ghost-hunting, entities at Fort Knox, and some of the families he said the team has helped. One could also find the book on Amazon.

Back in 2016, Jamie and his team used to annually raise between $3,000 and $6,000 for the fort.

Then as of October 2018, he was no longer taking on such paranormal cases. But instead, he started to take care of his own business. He had a jewelry store as well as his real estate agency.

However, in 2019, Jamie did show up on a TV show Paranormal Emergency, telling the audience his terrifying true tales of encounters with the paranormal while on duty.

Jamie Dube Age

Jamie Dube had turned 53 years old sometime before January 2022.

Is Jamie Dube Married?

Jamie Dube has been married to Jeanette Dube for some good time now.

So, Jamie’s wife Jeanette celebrates her birthday on the 5th of February every year. Professionally, she is an accounting manager at Northern Light Health. Also previously, she worked at Affiliated Healthcare Systems and before that studied at the University of Maine and went to Mt. Abram High School.

“On National Son’s day… Brady, I am so proud of you! You bring me so much joy and I love you so much!! Thanks for being you!!” Jeanette, a mother of two fondly wrote about her son Brady Dube.

Then, there’s Bryanna Dube, who as per her Facebook BIO, worked at Coach House Restaurant and studied at the University of Maine at Orono as of January 2022.

She also graduated alongside her cousin, Noah Dube, in 2019. Noah, if you should know, is Jamie’s brother Mike’s son who turned 20 in April 2021. He seemed to be the same age as Bryanna.

Jamie Dube reportedly had more than $1 million net worth as of January 2022.

Jamie Dube Height

Related FAQs

  • Where Is Jamie Dube From?

He often shares glimpses of his dad Butch, mom, and siblings on his social media.

Jamie grew up alongside his twin sister Julie Casburn and brother Mark Dube.

Proud of his Discovery stint, Jamie’s twin had also taken to her Facebook to appreciate him as “I am so proud of my twin!!!! Jamie started his journey many years ago and look at him now…he’s on The Discovery Channel. Check him out tonight at 10:00 p.m. on Mystery at Blind Frog Ranch. Again, so so proud of you Jamie! ”

Meanwhile, his brother has been a jeweler and working in the sales of Diamond & Gold Mine Outlet & Local Coupon Outlet for the past 18 years. So, it’s true that he and Jamie had collaborated in a jewelry business that they called Mark Jamie Dube.

One more thing. These Dube brothers also work in the real estate business for the same company, American Dream Realty LLC.; Jaime as an associated broker and Mark as a designated broker.
