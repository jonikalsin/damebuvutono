Alex Horne- Biography

Alex Horne was born on September 10, 1978, in England. Most notable for his satirical radio show The Horne Segment, Horne also contributed to the BBC Two TV program Never Mind The Buzzcocks and wrote and performed numerous pieces for the Edinburgh Fringe Festival.

At the end of 2012, his popular Horne Section comedy band performed a musical number that made fun of the comedian Josh Widdicombe. While studying for a Bachelor of Classics degree at Sidney Sus College, Cambridge, he worked with the Cambridge University Footlights Dramatic Club.
