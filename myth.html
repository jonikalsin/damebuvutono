Career

  • Ali first became interested in video games when he was four years old. On May 14, 2016, he became famous when he began broadcasting on Twitch. In his early days as a player, he used to broadcast Epic Games’ Paragon and Miscreated games on Twitch.
  • Ali turned to Fortnite when it was released and quickly became one of the most recognized and professional Fortnite players. He was also one of Twitch’s most followed streams after making Fortnite gaming content. Ali had just 800 Twitch followers before to playing Fortnite, but his fan following soared in the latter part of 2017. By the end of 2018, he had 3.8 million Twitch followers.
  • Myth debuted on YouTube on November 3, 2013, with the creation of his channel, which presently has 4.54 million followers. THE MIST – Miscreated Highlight was Ali’s debut YouTube video. On YouTube, he produces the same game material that he used to broadcast on Twitch. Ali is a part of Team SoloMid, which also includes Hamlinz, Daequan, and CaMiLLs.
  • Kabbani became captain of TSM in 2018. He competed in several esports competitions and titles. He competed in the Ninja Vegas Tournament in April 2018.
  • Ali routinely works with numerous notable gaming YouTubers in his gaming videos, like Pokimane and summit1g. However, during a live broadcast on December 28, 2021, Ali stated that he will not be renewing his contract with TSM.
