Casper Ruud siblings: Birk Ruud, Caroline Ruud, Charlotte Ruud

Casper Rudd is a professional Tennis player from Norway. He plays right-handed (two-handed backhand).

Having won seven titles and reaching ATP Tour Masters 1000 finals, Casper is credited as the first Norwegian to win an ATP title. Six of the titles he won were on clay courts.

Casper Ruud siblings: Birk Ruud, Caroline Ruud and Charlotte Ruud

Casper was born on December 22, 1998 (23 years) to Christian Rudd and Lele Rudd in Oslo, Norway.

Casper has two sisters and a brother. Not much is revealed about his siblings.

Casper’s father, Christian Rudd was also a professional tennis player , who is now serving as his coach.

The twenty-three year old player has a current singles rankings at World No. 7, attained on April 4, 2022. His current rank is the highest he has reached in his career.

For doubles, he’s ranking at World No. 211 since March 21, 2022. The highest doubles ranking he achieved was in July 2021 at No. 133.
